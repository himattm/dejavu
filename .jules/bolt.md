## 2024-04-27 - Jetpack Compose Stability Pattern\n**Learning:** In Compose, regular classes passed to Composable parameters use identity-based equality (Object.equals) instead of structural equality, causing them to trigger recompositions of children every time a parent recomposes, even if internal properties are identical. \n**Action:** Use `data class` for any parameter objects meant to be compared structurally to prevent redundant sub-tree recompositions.
